The Kurudamannil Kudumba Sangamam in Kuwait '02.

The initiative taken by a couple of Kurudamannil youngsters and their hard work paved the way to a Kudumba Sangamam on 15th November 2002. Sixty members and relatives of the Kurudamannil family in Kuwait attended this get together at Royal Auditorium in Abbassiya. The programme started, at 6:30 PM, with the song "Inneyolam thunachonne" followed by a prayer by Mr. Jacob George. Miss. Emy Rachel Mohan gave the welcome speech. A melodious song by Master Abin koshy and Bevan Koshy followed this. Mr. Boney K. Abraham gave the Presidential address, which was then followed by a song by Master Sachin K. Thomas. Mr. T. John Mathew gave a thought provoking speech based on Psalm 90. As, Dr. Thomas Easaw (Mohan) was leaving Kuwait after a few years of service it was decided to give a farewell to him. Mrs. Susan R. George presented Dr. Mohan with a memento and Mr. Ajit Mathew felicitated him on behalf of the Family members in Kuwait. Dr. Mohan ,in his reply, thanked all the family members. Later, after the departure of Dr. Mohan, the Sangamam recommenced with a song by Miss Shilpa Elizabeth Jacob. Then, Mr. George Verghese, the eldest member of the Kurudamannil Family in Kuwait, in his speech, gave vivid descriptions of Ayroor during his childhood days and these beautiful memories spread an air of nostalgia among the members on this cold winter evening. A melodious instrumental piece on organ by Miss Divya Susan followed the speech. Then came the introduction of the members and their relationship to the various Family branches. Mr. Cyrus Philip Ray kayipilakal gave vote of thanks and Dr. Binu Mathew led the closing prayer. The get together came to a close by 10 PM after a sumptuous dinner.
